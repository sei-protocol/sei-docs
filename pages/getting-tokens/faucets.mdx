# Faucets

## What is a Faucet?

Faucets distribute small amounts of tokens to enable developers and users to deploy and interact with contracts on testnets.

## Available Faucets

**For `arctic-1`**

- [NIMA Faucet](https://sei-faucet.nima.enterprises/)

For more faucet options, please visit our registry [here](https://github.com/sei-protocol/chain-registry/tree/main).
