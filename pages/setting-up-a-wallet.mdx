# Setting up a Wallet

## Installing a Wallet

You can navigate Sei with any of the following wallets:

**EVM & CosmWasm Compatible**

- [Compass](https://compasswallet.io/)
- [Fin](https://finwallet.com/)

**EVM Only**

- [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/)
- [Coinbase Wallet](https://www.coinbase.com/wallet)
- [Trust Wallet](https://trustwallet.com/)
- [Rainbow Wallet](https://rainbow.me/)

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  **Caution:** Importing your wallet from an EVM-only wallet to a
  dual-compatible wallet may result in a different address.
</Callout>
