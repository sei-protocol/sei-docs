# Setting up a Wallet

## Installing a Wallet

You can navigate Sei with any of the following wallets:

**EVM Only**

- [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/)

For more wallet options, please visit our registry [here](https://github.com/sei-protocol/chain-registry/blob/main/wallets.json).

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  **Caution:** Importing your wallet from an EVM-only wallet to a native Sei
  wallet, or vice versa, may result in a different address.
</Callout>
