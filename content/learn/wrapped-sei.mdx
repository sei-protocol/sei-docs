# Wrapping and Unwrapping SEI Tokens

This is a quick walk through of the process of wrapping and unwrapping SEI
tokens using the [DragonSwap](https://dragonswap.app/) interface, as well as
adding the token to your MetaMask wallet.

## Step 1: Connecting Your Wallet

First, ensure your wallet is connected. Most dashboards like this will show your
connected wallet address, or a button to connect the wallet app at the top-right
of the screen.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_select-mode.png" alt="Connected address and Wrap/Unwrap option" caption="Connected address and Wrap/Unwrap option" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

## Step 2: Selecting the Wrap/Unwrap Option

You can either use the "Wrap/Unwrap" button directly from the main menu or
select it from the swap menu.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_select-mode.png" alt="Selecting Wrap/Unwrap option" caption="Selecting Wrap/Unwrap option" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

## Step 3: Choosing the Token to Wrap

Select `WSEI` from the token list. If it is not listed, you can manually search
for it.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_select-wsei.png" alt="Select WSEI token" caption="Selecting WSEI token" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

## Step 4: Adding the Token to MetaMask

If this is your first time wrapping SEI, you need to add the `WSEI` token to
your MetaMask wallet. You will be prompted to add the token.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_wallet-add-wsei.png" alt="Add WSEI to MetaMask" caption="Adding WSEI to MetaMask" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

Confirm the addition of the `WSEI` token.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_wallet-add-confirm.png" alt="Confirm addition of WSEI token" caption="Confirming addition of WSEI token" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

## Step 5: Wrapping SEI

Now, enter the amount of SEI you want to wrap and confirm the transaction.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_select-amount.png" alt="Enter amount to wrap" caption="Entering amount to wrap" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_tx.png" alt="Wrap SEI transaction" caption="Wrapping SEI transaction" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

You can also access the wrapping function directly from the transaction menu.

## Step 6: Viewing the Wrapped Token

Once the transaction is confirmed, you can view your `WSEI` balance in your
MetaMask wallet.

<div className="my-4 flex flex-col items-center sm:flex-row justify-center gap-8 w-full">
  <ImageWithCaption img="https://cdn.sei.io/docs/assets/wrap_final.png" alt="View WSEI balance" caption="Viewing WSEI balance" className="max-w-xs mx-auto" />
</div>

## Unwrapping SEI

To unwrap `WSEI` back to `SEI`, follow the same steps but select the unwrap
option.

## Conclusion

Wrapping and unwrapping SEI tokens is a straightforward process. Ensure you have
the correct token added to your wallet and follow the steps for wrapping or
unwrapping as needed.
