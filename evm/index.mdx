## Why choose Sei EVM?

| Feature                    | Sei EVM       | Ethereum         | Other Layer 1s  |
| -------------------------- | ------------- | ---------------- | --------------- |
| Blocktime                  | 400 ms        | 12 – 14 seconds  | 1 – 3 seconds   |
| Transaction Throughput     | 100 MGas/s    | 3 MGas/s         | < 50 MGas/s     |
| Parallelized Execution     | Yes           | No               | Limited / No    |
| EVM Tooling Compatibility  | 100%          | 100%             | Varies          |
| EVM Version                | Pectra (w/o blobs) | Fusaka      | Varies          |

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Sei to your wallet">
    Install the [Sei Global Wallet](/evm/sei-global-wallet) or configure [network details](/evm/networks) in MetaMask, Rabby, or any EVM-compatible wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get test tokens">
    Request testnet SEI from the [faucet](/learn/faucet) and review [supported tokens](/evm/tokens) on Sei mainnet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start building">
    Follow the [EVM general guide](/evm/evm-general) or jump straight into [project templates](https://github.com/sei-protocol/sei-chain/tree/main/example).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## RPC endpoints

Choose from official Sei Foundation endpoints or community-maintained alternatives. All endpoints support standard EVM JSON-RPC methods. Public endpoints have rate limits — for production apps, consider using a [dedicated RPC provider](/learn/rpc-providers) or [running your own node](/node).
